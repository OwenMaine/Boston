About Us

Premier Trailer Manufacturing & Rental Services

Boston Trailer Manufacturing Company, established in 2004 by Mark Warsofsky and his son Jarod, has grown to become a leading provider of trailer rentals, leasing, and sales in New England. Headquartered at 1 Production Road in Walpole, Massachusetts, the company has expanded its operations across multiple locations throughout the region, managing a fleet of over 4,000 trailers. BOSTON TRAILER The company's product offerings are diverse, catering to a wide range of customer needs. They provide road and storage dry vans of various sizes, liftgates, refrigerated units, flatbeds, and construction trailers. To support their clients, Boston Trailer offers 24/7 roadside assistance, mobile maintenance services, shop repairs for private fleets, GPS tracking, and custom company decals. Their fleet of private road service trucks operates throughout the New England region, ensuring prompt and efficient service. In addition to their rental and leasing services, Boston Trailer also offers both new and used trailers for sale, including flatbed and dry vans, refrigeration units, and chassis trailers. They exclusively sell road trailers and dry vans manufactured by industry leaders, such as Vanguard National Trailer Corporation, ensuring reliability and performance for their customers. The Walpole facility serves as a central hub for the company's operations, specializing in the production and servicing of vehicle trailers. One of their significant clients is U-Haul, for whom they build and service trailers. The facility's strategic location off Main Street in Walpole allows for easy accessibility and efficient distribution of their products and services. Boston Trailer has also established partnerships with various dealers to enhance their offerings. For instance, they collaborate with Contral Trailers, founded in 1995 in Savannah, Georgia, by Allen Engel. Contral Trailers has innovated the way drivers deliver containers under diverse conditions, providing trailers built to move 10’, 20’, or 40’ containers, and adaptable for hauling various freight such as vehicles, materials, and light equipment. The company's commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is evident in their comprehensive range of services and products. Their mobile maintenance units are fully equipped for expert trailer repair, routine maintenance, and Department of Transportation (D.O.T.) inspections. Offering 24/7 dispatching of maintenance trucks, they ensure that clients' operations continue smoothly with minimal downtime. Over the years, Boston Trailer has solidified its reputation as a trusted provider in the trailer industry within New England. Their dedication to service excellence, quality equipment, and continuous expansion of their product offerings underscore their position as a leader in the region. As they continue to grow, their focus remains on meeting the evolving needs of their customers and maintaining the high standards that have become synonymous with their name.
